Our Mission

About
VakilAI

"Legal clarity is not a privilege — it is a right. We make it accessible to every Indian citizen."

India has over 50 million pending court cases. A large part of this burden exists because ordinary citizens — tenants, property buyers, loan borrowers — receive legal documents they simply cannot understand.

VakilAI was built to solve this. Upload any Indian legal document and receive a plain-language explanation in your regional language within seconds. No legal background required. No lawyer fees. No jargon.

Built by a student from Kolkata, for 800 million working Indians who deserve to understand what they sign.

WHAT WE DO

We use AI to read your legal documents and explain them in simple language — your obligations, key dates, red flags, and what to do next.

WHAT WE DON'T DO

We do not provide legal advice. VakilAI helps you understand documents — for actual legal action, always consult a qualified lawyer or your nearest DLSA.

YOUR PRIVACY

Your documents are processed in real-time and never stored permanently without your consent. We do not share your data with any third party.
